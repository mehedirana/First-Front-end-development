My School

The name of my school is Rasulpur govt high school. It is in Gafargaon Thana under Mymensing district. There are five hundred students and eighteen teachers. There are 15 rooms in the school. Thirteen rooms are for classes, one room is for the teachers and the other is for the Head Teacher. The results of the school is good. All the teachers of our school are very friendly and helpful. They are high qualified teacher. They teach us with pleasure. They loves us like their own children. There is a big play ground in front of the school. I love my school very much

My country

The name of our country is Bangladesh. It is situated in the south Asia. Its land area is 147570 square kilometers with a large number of populations. It became independence on 16th December 1971 from Pakistan. Dhaka is the capital of this country.

My Mother

My mother is the best mom in the world. Her name is Amanda. She is only 25 years old. She is very hard working women. She is a housewife. She gets up early in the morning and makes breakfast for us. She takes care of my Papa, Grandfathers, grandmothers, and sister. Everybody in my family loves and appreciates her. Really, she deserves it.
